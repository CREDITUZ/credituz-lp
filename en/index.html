Our methodology

From diagnosis to measured ROI. In 4 steps.

Reserved for Enterprise accounts. A senior team runs your operation from initial mapping to continuous optimization, with KPIs agreed in the contract, weekly milestones and a named Customer Success manager tracking each improvement cycle.

01

Mystery Shopper · Diagnosis

We act as a mystery shopper in your operation for 7 days: we pose as a real buyer across the channels defined with you, matching your business reality. We time the response, map lead leakage and assess qualification, simulation quality and conversion bottlenecks by channel and by agent.

Step 1 · Deliverable: opportunity report quantified in R$
02

Pipeline setup

We import your lead base and portfolio, configure CRM, multi-bank simulation, digital contracts, e-invoicing, an Asaas digital account and templates in your brand voice. We connect to Sienge, CV CRM, Kenlo or your current ERP via API with two-way sync. On-site onboarding for the sales and finance teams.

Step 2 · Deliverable: platform live and team trained
03

AI live

Sofia (AI SDR) goes live on WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook and web chat with 24/7 coverage. Automated collection flows by portfolio (monthly, in-house financing, transfers) start running. The first 30 days include active human supervision, prompt fine-tuning and CS review of every conversation to ensure tone and qualification rate.

Step 3 · Deliverable: AI SDR + collection flows in production
04

Continuous optimization

A named Customer Success manager tracks weekly metrics and a monthly executive report: conversion by bank, recovery by channel, average ticket, time to close and payback. We iterate prompts, templates, collection cascades and channel mix each cycle. KPIs reviewed in a QBR (Quarterly Business Review) with your leadership.

Step 4 · Deliverable: KPIs measured, ROI proven, continuous improvement

Frequently asked questions

What people ask most.

Does it work with my current CRM?

+
Yes. Credituz imports contacts from your current CRM in 1 click. Native integration with ZAP Imóveis, OLX and VivaReal is available from the Pro plan (with real-time two-way sync). On Free and Starter, you add leads manually or via import. You can run it in parallel while migrating or let Credituz fully replace your system, which is what most end up doing.

How much does it cost to start?

+
Zero. No credit card. The Free plan is free forever and includes the credit CRM, unlimited multi-bank simulation, a sales landing page for 1 development and active origination. The AI agents (like Sofia) come with the paid plans, from Starter up, and you can try them free for 14 days on any plan except Enterprise.

How does the annual discount work?

+
You pay a single annual amount and get about 29% off on all paid plans. For example, Pro at R$ 597/month works out to R$ 424/month equivalent when billed annually. It is the recommended option once you have validated the product and want to lock in the best price.

How do you make money if the plan is free?

+
Two sources: (1) subscriptions on the paid plans (Starter, Growth, Pro, Business and Enterprise), and (2) commission from partner banks when a financing we originated is approved. Aligned incentives: we only earn when you close a deal.

Is the AI good in Portuguese?

+
Yes, trained on Brazilian real estate vocabulary. It understands audio, images and regional slang. Clients chatting with Sofia often don't realize they are talking to an AI.

Will my team be able to use it?

+
Setup in 5 minutes, a WhatsApp/Notion-style interface everyone already knows, and no scheduled training. Free and Starter include a knowledge base and video tutorials. From Pro up, you get human chat support Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm. Business adds dedicated customer success. Enterprise adds a named CS manager and on-site onboarding.

How does the 14-day free trial work?

+
All paid plans (Starter, Growth, Pro and Business) include a 14-day free trial with full access, including AI agents like Sofia. You can cancel the trial anytime. The Free plan stays free forever, with CRM, multi-bank simulation and contracts, but without the AI agents. Enterprise, given its custom scope, starts with a guided demo instead of a trial.

What's the difference between Sofia and Meta's free AI agent (Business AI)?

+
Meta's agent answers questions based on the catalog: it is a generic assistant, and Meta itself has announced it will become paid. Sofia is vertical to real estate: she simulates financing across 10 banks, qualifies the lead with pre-approved credit, schedules visits, updates the CRM on her own and triggers contracts and collections. Meta's agent ends the conversation; Sofia closes the sale. And the two can coexist: Meta handles the curious, Sofia handles the buyer.
